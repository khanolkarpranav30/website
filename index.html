Research Experience

Graduate Research Assistant - Ready Lab, University of Toronto

(Sept 2020 -present)

Under the supervision of Prof. Alison Olechowski at Ready Lab , I worked towards understanding methods that help enhance product design processes espicially through the lens of AI and automation.

Implementing AI/automation in Industry:

Collaborated with an industry-design-team to understand process behind the selection, development and implementation of an apt automation framework for improving the efficiency of their product design process.

I drafted project proposal for Mitacs Accelerate Research Grant. In this project, I developed and deployed a framework that automated the design and manufacturing-specification-generation processes of FRP flanges, that proved to be 7.5 times faster than their traditional design process

Mapping AI to Engineering Design Stages:

Conducted a focused literature review to highlight to status quo of seven different AI-based methods focused towards five engineering design stages.

In this project, I reviewed several state-of-th-art AI-based design research works and classified them to five design stages (problem definition, conceptual design, preliminary design, detailed desig, and design communication), based on the AI-method applied.

Exploring AI Education in Engineering Design Curricula:

Analyzed the current prevalance of AI courses across mechanical engineering curriula of Canadian accredited universities.

In this project, I developed a novel "web-scraping + keyword-matching" approach to analyze the current prevalance of AI courses across mechanical engineering curriula (~1700 courses) of 28 Canadian accredited universities.


Graduate Research Assistant - THRED Group, The Pennsylvania State University

(June 2019 - May 2020)

Under the supervision of Prof. Christopher McComb (now at Carnegie Mellon University) at THRED Group, I worked towards understanding how deep learning algorithms can help accelerate structural evaluations of materials.

Applying Deep Learning for Material Evaluation :

Conducted strain field material evaluations of porosity-ridden material microstructures using deep learning-based image colorization algorithm.

In this project, I developed a framework that leveraged deep neural network to predict strain fields in aluminium microstructures with 95% accuracy and 15x faster than traditional FEA software (ABAQUS).

Other Projects

In addition to the major projects, I completed the following projects as part of my research and education.

Comparative Analysis of Self-Supervised Learners :

Performed qualitive analysis using T-SNE plots and on these five self-supervised learning algorithms using CIFAR10 dataset and and quantitative analysis of these self-supervised learning (SSL) algorithms by evaluating their models’ transfer learning ability using out-of-distribution testing for image classification.

Statistical analyses of the BRFSS for effective monitoring of weight related concerns:

Analyzed the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) dataset, using K-means clustering for pattern identification and subsequently logitsic regression to predict heart-attack-diagnosis, cancer-diagnosis, and diabetes-diagnosis.

Remote Order Taking – Discrete Event Simulation :

Analyzed and optimized the number of servers required for a drive-through restaurant- chain in the area to improve efficiency according to desired performance standards using discrete event simulations in Python.

Non-Destructive Testing of Thin Plates using Ultrasonic Guided Waves :

Developed two new algorithms for damage detection, localization, and refinement using lamb waves.
